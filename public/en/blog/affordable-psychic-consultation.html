Why do psychic readings cost so much elsewhere?

The online psychic world often runs on a per-minute model. You call, the clock starts, and the conversation sometimes stretches on deliberately. The result: a simple question can cost you anywhere from €20 to €100, depending on how long the call runs and which platform you use. Not to mention premium-rate numbers, automatic callbacks and "introductory offers" that turn into ongoing commitments.

That model doesn't sit right with me. I don't charge per minute, there's no subscription, no premium-rate number. A fixed price, stated clearly before payment. Full stop.

Affordable psychic readings: traps to avoid elsewhere

If you're looking for accessible psychic guidance online, stay alert. Here are some red flags to watch for:

  • Beware of "10 free minutes" that turn into 45 minutes billed at top rate.
  • Avoid automatic subscriptions and top-up credit systems.
  • Steer clear of premium-rate numbers where the per-minute cost is never clearly shown upfront.
  • Don't trust psychics who call you back to encourage you to consult again.
  • A legitimate psychic doesn't need to frighten you to keep you as a client. Guidance illuminates — it doesn't threaten.

At Aura Intuitive, the model is the exact opposite of all that. No callbacks, no follow-ups, no pressure. You come when you feel the need, and you leave with your answer. That's it.
