Laura, medium and intuitive psychic reader

Behind Aura Intuitive, there is a person. An intuition. And the conviction that every soul deserves sincere, compassionate guidance.

Who am I?

My name is Laura, and I have been a medium since childhood. For as far back as I can remember, I have always perceived things others could not see: feelings about people, impressions about situations, a sensitivity I spent years trying to understand before accepting it and placing it at the service of others.

I believe deeply that every person is unique, and that every situation — whether in love, friendship, or work — is equally unique. That is why I place the greatest importance on compassion and non-judgement in every reading I offer.

My vision of spiritual reading

To me, a psychic reading is the analysis of a situation or a feeling, at a precise moment in time, which remains open to change. In my view, nothing is set in stone: free will is the master of the game, and you are the master of your destiny.

My intuitions and feelings are here to guide you and show you the range of possibilities that each choice opens up, within a specific context. This is not about guessing games, making promises, or offering flattering readings that tell you only what you want to hear.

It is about understanding you, helping you find the light, choosing the path that is best for you, and offering you the most sincere support I can give.

Why Aura Intuitive?

I created Aura Intuitive because I believe in honest, accessible spiritual guidance for everyone. Too many platforms charge by the minute, with prices that soar, hidden subscriptions, and generic answers. That is not my vision.

Here, pricing is as accessible as possible, because I do not believe in profiting from people's vulnerability. What matters is accompanying you with kindness, transparency, and genuine support. No subscription, no saved card details, no follow-up pressure. Just a reading, an answer, and the freedom to come back whenever you feel the need.

My method

All my readings are delivered by email. This format allows me to take the time to connect with your energy, craft my response with care, and offer you a message you can read and re-read as many times as you need.

  • Yes / No Answer (€1): you ask a precise question, I connect with your energy, and you receive a clear intuitive answer within 24 hours.
  • Intuitive Reading (€10): you describe your situation, and I send you a detailed, personalised reading with my feelings, the dynamics I perceive, and guiding insights.

Every response is written personally by me. There are no pre-written templates, no automated replies. It is my intuition, my words — for you.
