Frequently asked questions

◆
Is payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed by Stripe, the world's leading online payment provider. Your bank details are never stored on our site. The connection is HTTPS-encrypted.

How soon will I receive my answer?

You will receive your personalised response by email within 24 hours of submitting your question. Please check your spam folder.

What is the difference between the two services?

The Intuitive Reading (€10) is detailed guidance with full impressions, emotional energy and timing. The Yes / No Answer (€1) is a short, intuitive response to one specific question, without timing.

Is my personal data protected?

Absolutely. Your information remains strictly confidential. It is never shared, sold or used for any purpose other than your reading.

Can I ask multiple questions?

Yes! Each payment corresponds to one question. You can book as many readings as you wish.

Does a psychic reading replace medical advice?

No, not at all. A psychic reading is spiritual and intuitive guidance. It does not replace medical, psychological or legal advice. Health-related questions are not handled.

Are there any hidden fees or a subscription?

Absolutely not. Affordable psychic readings here means no tricks: you pay €1 for a Yes/No Answer or €10 for a full Intuitive Reading. No subscription, no automatic renewal, no extra charges. You pay once, you receive your answer. That's it.

Payment issue or I haven't received my answer?

If you have an issue with your payment or haven't received your email response (please check your spam folder), contact me directly on TikTok: @aura_intuitive. I will get back to you as soon as possible! 🙏
